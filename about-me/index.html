--- title: About me layout: page ---

I’m a self-taught Swedish developer, brought up on an island named Resarö in the Stockholm archipelago. My history includes a short visit to Jönköping University where I met my partner Elin, a pit stop at Gothenburg University, and then roughly five years as a developer at Elabs (Varvet since 2016).

In 2016 Elin and I moved to Stockholm to get closer to family. Today we live happily out in the woods of Blackeberg, Stockholm. I continue to work as a developer and founder of a small consumer-app company; and whenever I can I will wakeboard, snowboard, play computer games, and juggle.

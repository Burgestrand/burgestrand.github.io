I’m a self-taught Swedish developer, brought up on an island named Resarö in the Stockholm archipelago. My history includes a small detour to meet my spouse Elin in Jönköping, a flyby of Jönköping University, a short pit stop at Gothenburg University, and roughly five years as a developer at Elabs.

Today Elin and I live happily out in the woods of Blackeberg, Stockholm. I still work as a developer (now with Sqore) and whenever I can I will wakeboard, snowboard, play computer games, and juggle — however not at all the same time.

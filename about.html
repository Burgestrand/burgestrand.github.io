I’m a Swedish developer, brought up on an island named Resarö in the Stockholm archipelago. For almost five years I worked in Gothenburg at a little place called Elabs, but in 2016 I decided to move back to Stockholm since I missed my friends & family.

My girlfriend Elin and I now live happily in Stockholm, out in the woods in Blackeberg. I still work as a developer, and quite enjoy it. Whenever I can I will wakeboard, snowboard, play computer games, and juggle — however not at the same time.
